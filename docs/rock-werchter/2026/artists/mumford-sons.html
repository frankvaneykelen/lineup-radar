Background

Mumford & Sons is a British band known for their blend of folk, rock, and bluegrass influences, characterized by their use of traditional instruments like the banjo and their anthemic, emotionally resonant songs. They achieved global success with albums such as 'Sigh No More' and 'Babel,' the latter winning the Grammy Award for Album of the Year in 2013.
