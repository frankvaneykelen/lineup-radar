About (from Festival)

Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun from Columbus, Ohio, make up Twenty One Pilots. The dynamic duo have shifted music and culture as an incomparable creative force and with an ever-evolving vision. They have created a unique universe in which the main character, Clancy, tries to escape the fictional city of Dema and overcome his inner demons, represented by Blurryface. It is a symbolic story about mental health that spans multiple albums. The music, visuals, and mysterious narratives form an immersive world that captivates fans around the globe. Blurryface is also the title of their breakthrough album, which set a remarkable record: every track achieved gold or platinum status. In 2024, they released their seventh album, Clancy. Even before the accompanying tour was completed, they announced a new album. The recently released Breach serves as the epilogue to the mythical story of Clancy, which began more than ten years ago. With over 33 billion streams, more than three million tickets sold, dozens of platinum singles, and even a mention in the Guinness® World Records book for the longest music video ever, Twenty One Pilots remain one of the most innovative and intriguing acts of their generation. Catch them on Saturday 4 July on the Main Stage!
