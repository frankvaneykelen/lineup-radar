Formed in 2024, Tracey is a London-based duo creating mutant R&B unlike anyone else. Imagine booty bass dance music lathered with a frothy layer of sardonic British playfulness; think Miami via Millwall. Their eponymous debut EP arrived as a surprise in the summer of 2025, like a lower-back tattoo after a night on the town. Although it's frontloaded with the undeniable banger "Sex Life (feat. Riko Dan)," later in the EP, Tracey reveal their careful, slow, and atmospheric side: from the downtempo brilliance of "When I Choose to Be Here with You," to the touching emo-infused R&B of closer "Take Care," Tracey are full of surprises. Followup *Sleazy* (2025) returns to the hyperactive dance arena, borrowing too from PC Music with its colourful acoustics and tight sample style (which, like "Sex Life," has a satisfying Benassi bounciness to its vocal chops). Raunchy yet thoughtful, bassy yet subtle, Tracey create an anomaly of sounds, likely informed by the eclectic influences they cite: among them, Kylie Minogue, Skream, and Crystal Castles.
