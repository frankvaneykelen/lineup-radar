Her collaboration with ONCEIM (Orchestre de Nouvelles Créations, Expérimentations et Improvisation Musicales) explores the intersection of electronic and orchestral music, creating immersive and hypnotic soundscapes.

Festival Bio (English)

On Rewire 2026's opening evening at Amare, Italian composer and artist Caterina Barbieri will present a new collaboration with ONCEIM – the Orchestra of New Musical Creation, Experimentation, and Improvisation. Barbieri has written a new composition for the orchestra and will perform it with them. Barbieri has long channeled her curiosity into a cosmic mosaic of electronic works touching on themes of memory, time, and perception. Focussing around generative techniques and the creative potential of computation, and using vivid modular synthesis and expanded electro-acoustic formats, Barbieri examines states of trance and altered consciousness. Her approach to music is surely theoretically grounded, yet her manifold compositions and works are also deeply intuitive. She treats her compositions as living organisms which are developed and altered through live performance. Led by artistic and musical director Frédéric Blondy, ONCEIM is an orchestra of over thirty musicians from a variety of musical backgrounds, including improvised music, jazz, classical and contemporary music, and experimental music.
