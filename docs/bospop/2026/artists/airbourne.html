Festival Bio (English)

Airbourne makes rock like only Australians can: hard, steadfast, and straight to the point. Since brothers Joel and Ryan O’Keeffe started the band in 2003, Airbourne has grown into a global live sensation, racking up more than 1,100 shows and appearances at festivals like Wacken, Download, Hellfest, and Rock am Ring. With albums like ‘Runnin’ Wild,’ ‘No Guts. No Glory.,’ and ‘Breakin’ Outta Hell,’ they’ve stormed charts worldwide and won the Classic Rock Award for Best Live Band. Their sound channels the spirit of AC/DC and Motörhead, but always with their own relentless drive. Expect fire, volume, and pure hard rock euphoria: Airbourne isn’t here to whisper, they’re here to ignite.
