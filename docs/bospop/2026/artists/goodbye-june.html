Festival Bio (English)

Goodbye June combines raw hard rock riffs with southern blues and gospel influences, driven by the desire to create timeless, spirited rock anthems. The three cousins formed the band after the loss of lead guitarist Tyler's brother in June, determined to honor his memory. Since their debut album 'Magic Valley,' the band has received praise from the renowned music magazine Rolling Stone, provided music for major brands like Budweiser and WWE, and toured with Greta Van Fleet and ZZ Top. With over 110 million Spotify streams and features on ESPN, NFL, NHL, and games like Need for Speed and Madden, Goodbye June has firmly established themselves. Their album 'See Where the Night Goes' reached the #1 spot on the UK Rock Chart, followed by the 2024 album 'Deep in the Trouble.'
