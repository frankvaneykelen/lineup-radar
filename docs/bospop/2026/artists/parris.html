Festival Bio (English)

Parris is the Dutch Thin Lizzy tribute band dedicated to keeping this musical spirit alive. They stand out with their immense enthusiasm, energy, and love for this music. The combination of musical craftsmanship and a singer who ranks high on the list of forbidden weapons has proven to be highly fruitful. Parris has had the honor of performing multiple times at The Vibe for Philo, the ultimate Thin Lizzy event worldwide, held annually in memory of frontman Phil Lynott. Additionally, they can be heard on the 2001 double album Tribute To The Black Rose, which also features contributions from members of Uriah Heep and Bachman-Turner Overdrive. You can't get closer to Thin Lizzy than this!
