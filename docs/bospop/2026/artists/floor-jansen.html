Bio

Floor Jansen is a Dutch singer and songwriter best known as the lead vocalist of the symphonic metal band Nightwish. Renowned for her powerful and versatile voice, she has also fronted the bands After Forever and ReVamp, earning critical acclaim for her dynamic performances and contributions to the metal genre.
