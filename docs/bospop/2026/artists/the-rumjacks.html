Festival Bio (English)

The Rumjacks were formed in Sydney in 2008 and combine punk rock with influences from traditional folk. After their early years in Australia, the band relocated to Europe in 2016 to tour extensively, which has since led to performances at countless festivals and venues across Europe and the United Kingdom. With tracks like ‘An Irish Pub Song,’ the group gained a large international following and secured a permanent spot within the Celtic punk scene. The Rumjacks are known for their crowd-pulling performances, emphasizing sing-alongs, high energy, and a powerful live presence.
