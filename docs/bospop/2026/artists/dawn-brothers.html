Festival Bio (English)

Dawn Brothers is devoid of frills and has long been a band you can rely on. Watching the Rotterdam quartet live is to admire craftsmanship – a creation of folk rock, blues, and soul that leaves no room for doubt. Don’t expect grand sing-along moments or flashy stage antics. Dawn Brothers is like a flawless piece of furniture, crafted out of pure love for music. The same goes for their new studio album ‘Cry Alone,’ where the band continues to explore no-nonsense new techniques and gives their spicy Americana sound a slightly darker edge. With Dawn Brothers, there is always room for experimentation, new themes, and emotions, both on record and on stage.
