Festival Bio (English)

The American blues singer and guitarist D.K. Harrell first picked up the harmonica at the age of eleven and started playing guitar a few years later. He immersed himself completely in the music of B.B. King. In 2019, he landed his first paid gig as a musician at a symposium at the B.B. King Museum, where he even had the honor of playing on The King's beloved Gibson 'Lucille' guitar. Since then, Harrell has forged ahead with determination. His debut album, *The Right Man*, received rave reviews in 2023, and in 2024 he was honored as Best New Artist at the prestigious Blues Music Awards. With D.K. Harrell, the future of blues is in excellent hands.
