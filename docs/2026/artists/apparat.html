Background

Apparat, the stage name of Sascha Ring, is a German electronic musician and producer known for blending ambient, experimental, and techno elements in his music. He has gained critical acclaim for his solo work as well as collaborations with Modeselektor in the project Moderat.

About (from Festival)

Sascha Ring emerged about 25 years ago in the Berlin dance scene, at a time when the German capital was entirely dominated by minimal techno. As Apparat, he infused that restrained genre with more complex rhythms and deeper emotions. Since then, he has been synonymous with melodic and captivating electronic music, effortlessly navigating between ambient, pop, and techno. Together with the members of Modeselektor, he formed the supergroup Moderat, delivering dance music on a blockbuster scale. And as a solo project, Apparat remains a synonym for immersive and innovative dance music that moves and overwhelms.
