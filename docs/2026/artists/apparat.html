About (from Festival)

Sascha Ring emerged about 25 years ago in Berlin's dance scene, at a time when the German capital was dominated by minimal techno. As Apparat, he infused that understated genre with more complex rhythms and deeper emotions. Since then, he has been synonymous with melodic and captivating electronic music, effortlessly navigating between ambient, pop, and techno. Together with the members of Modeselektor, he formed the supergroup Moderat, delivering dance music on a blockbuster scale. And as a solo project, Apparat remains a synonym for immersive and innovative dance music that moves and overwhelms.
