About (from Festival)

They surfaced through TikTok and Insta, took the stage at Noorderslag 2025, worked their way through the national club circuit, and toured Europe, both on their own and with artists like Abby Sage, Flyte, and MARO. And all of this with just a handful of intimate pop tracks to their name, which have already racked up nearly 250,000 streams. Not bad for two students from the Rotterdam music academy Codarts. But it all makes sense once Claudia and Loeki unleash their heavenly harmony folk vocals on you.
