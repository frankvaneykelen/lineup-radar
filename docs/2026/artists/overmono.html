About (from Festival)

They fit seamlessly into the exclusive lineup of British electronic live duos like The Chemical Brothers, Orbital, and Disclosure. Like the latter two, Overmono also consists of actual brothers—Ed and Tom, to be precise—which they share in common with the first. Their musical repertoire offers a variety of familiar touchpoints: from breakbeats and drum-’n-bass to UK garage and techno. Not a throwaway sound, but irresistibly danceable.
