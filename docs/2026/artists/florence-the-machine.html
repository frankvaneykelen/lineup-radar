Background

Florence + The Machine is a British band led by Florence Welch, known for their powerful vocals, poetic lyrics, and a blend of indie rock and baroque pop. They have achieved critical and commercial success with albums like 'Lungs' and 'Ceremonials,' earning multiple awards and nominations.
