About (from Festival)

The glory days of MTV flash before your eyes when you hear the grunge-pop of Florence Road. Their debut mixtape ‘Fall Back’ is bursting with gems that have raw, edgy charm. The four-piece group of friends from Ireland, led by vocalist Lily Aron, quickly emerged as an indie sensation. They started as a cover band on TikTok and have already opened for Olivia Rodrigo. Now, the rock band is working on their first album while taking the festival circuit by storm with their emotional sing-alongs.
