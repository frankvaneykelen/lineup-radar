About (from Festival)

Nick Littlemore and Luke Steele, with noble playfulness, do exactly what they feel like, whether sparring in studios with Jay-Z, Beyoncé, Dua Lipa, or Elton John. The main condition: it has to be danceable, especially now that the electropop duo returns to the festival stages with their fourth album, ‘Ask That God’ (2024). The Talk Talk bassline in ‘Changes,’ the butterflies of infatuation in ‘The Feeling You Get,’ the occasionally Bee Gees-like vocal craftsmanship, and the extravagant, unparalleled live spectacle of Lord Littlemore and Emperor Steele: Empire Of The Sun effortlessly activates your happy self.
