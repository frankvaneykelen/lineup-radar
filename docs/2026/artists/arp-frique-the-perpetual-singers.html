About (from Festival)

Inject soulful gospel with a hefty dose of ecstatic P-Funk, and the addictive result will come dangerously close to Arp Frique & The Perpetual Singers. The Amsterdam-based multi-instrumentalist Arp Frique composed his latest album, 'The Gospel of Jesamy,' as a danceable tribute to his newborn daughter. Supported by an exceptionally talented band and legendary vocalists like Rocq-E Harrell—who has worked with the likes of Stevie Wonder and Earth, Wind & Fire—he translates this intimate opus into an ecstatic funk celebration on stage.
