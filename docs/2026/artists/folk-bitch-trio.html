About (from Festival)

Seize the day, start a band, thought Heide, Jeanie, and Gracie, to capture their everyday ups and downs in melodies as the Folk Bitch Trio. By now, little remains ordinary for these Melbourne minstrels. Especially with their analog-recorded debut album, which awakens your eardrums with angelic, sometimes a cappella high-level harmonies and refined guitar chords versus sharply crafted lyrics. The album title beautifully encapsulates how a close friendship can grow into a globally touring folk group, simply by deciding together: ‘Now Would Be A Good Time.’
