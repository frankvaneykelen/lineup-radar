About (from Festival)

Who says country is dead? CMAT has been proving the opposite for over three albums now. The Irish singer-songwriter revamps the classic Nashville sound with razor-sharp and humorous songs that shed light on the world's woes. Her latest album, ‘EURO-COUNTRY,’ is not only a perfect description of her unique musical style but also a passionate indictment of the capitalism that has hollowed out Ireland. Honest and fearless, that's Ciara Mary-Alice Thompson, especially on stage, where she kicks against all sacred cows with a wide grin. Photo by Sarah Doyle
