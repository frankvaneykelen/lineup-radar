Background

Little Simz is a British rapper, singer, and actress known for her introspective lyrics, innovative production, and critically acclaimed albums such as 'Sometimes I Might Be Introvert' and 'Grey Area'. She has won multiple awards, including the Mercury Prize and a BRIT Award, solidifying her position as one of the most influential voices in contemporary hip-hop.
