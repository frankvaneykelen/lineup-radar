Background

Matt Berninger is the lead vocalist of the critically acclaimed indie rock band The National, known for his deep baritone voice and introspective lyrics. He has also released solo work, including his debut album 'Serpentine Prison' in 2020, which explores themes of melancholy and personal reflection.
