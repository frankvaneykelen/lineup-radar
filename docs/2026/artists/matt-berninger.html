About (from Festival)

If anyone were ever to fall victim to Stendhal syndrome through music—mentally and physically overwhelmed by beauty—then this soul-stirring baritone must be the cause. He overcame writer's block and depression, penned lyrics for two The National albums, and dusted off the beginnings of his solo record, ‘Get Sunk.’ Text ideas scribbled on baseballs turned into melancholic yet hopeful home runs in collaboration with producer and fellow songwriter Sean O'Brien. In ‘Times of Difficulty,’ there’s only one person you want to listen to: Matt Berninger. Photo by Chantal Anderson
