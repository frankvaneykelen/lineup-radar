About (from Festival)

Centuries-old folk music from Anatolia wrapped in a delightful psychedelic vibe—that’s the sound of Derya Yıldırım and her phenomenal band Grup Şimşek. The Hamburg-born multi-instrumentalist with Anatolian roots describes her music as "outernational," transcending the boundaries of countries and genres. You can feel it when she takes the stage with her bağlama, a seven-string Turkish lute: past and present merge during electrifying performances where the rich tradition of Turkish folk sounds more relevant than ever. Preservation and innovation go hand in hand here. Photo by Philomena Wolflingseder
