Mon dieu! The most played French-language track on French radio and TV was created by a Canadian? With 'Feel Good' (2024), Charlotte Cardin from Montréal firmly set foot on the European mainland and far beyond. In her suitcase, she brings sublime alternative pop, continuously surprising with perfectly balanced synths, beats, and guitars.
