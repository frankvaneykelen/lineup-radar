About (from Festival)

Service announcement: Celtic folk is alive and well and living in The Mary Wallopers. Not in the form of a staid old-man pub vibe, but in the spirit of the mighty Pogues (may God rest their souls): raw and straight from the heart. Pure Irish rock ’n’ roll. Built around the Hendy brothers and authentically equipped with tin whistle and uilleann pipes, the band from Dundalk celebrates life in all its facets, boldly taking a stand against all that is wrong—far-right extremism, the genocide in Gaza, capitalism, and whatnot.
