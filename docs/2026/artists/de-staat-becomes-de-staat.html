About (from Festival)

DTRH house friends De Staat are known as one of Europe’s most inventive and fearless live acts. For over a quarter of a century, they have consistently pushed the boundaries of what a rock band can be. They combine razor-sharp social criticism with a unique visual stage language – ‘Witch Doctor,’ anyone? – and a genre-defying sound that, besides dominating airplay, has also been featured in hit games and global campaigns. Just take a look at their trophy cabinet, which gleams with accolades like Edison Awards, 3FM Awards, and a Bernays Lifetime Achievement Award. All of this has taken the internationally renowned band to all corners of the globe: from SXSW, Glastonbury, Lowlands, Sziget, Pukkelpop, Rock Werchter, and DEICHBRAND to tours with bands like The Rolling Stones, Muse, and Biffy Clyro. In 2026, Torre Florim and his companions will return with "De Staat Becomes De Staat," a grand new project centered around a fresh album, live show, and a massive art installation that will once again raise the bar. Unthinkable? With De Staat, you can even expect the impossible to become possible.
