About (from Festival)

She had been out of sight for a while, Joy Crookes, the London singer of Bengali-Irish descent who debuted in 2021 with a stunning neo-soul album and was already admired at Down The Rabbit Hole a year later. But now she’s back. That life hasn’t always smiled upon her in the meantime is evident from the emotional lyrics on ‘Juniper’ (2025), an album that continues where its predecessor left off with retro soul infused with hints of trip-hop, rap, and reggae. Jazzier than Amy Winehouse, more hip-hop than Celeste, a true ode to Joy.
