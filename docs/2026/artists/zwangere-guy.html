About (from Festival)

This reborn Brussels rapper transitioned from his debut 'Wie is Guy?' to the new release 'Dit is Guy.'—a mental obstacle course of ups and downs—and found the answers he was seeking. Goodbye booze and drugs, hello healthy lifestyle and daughter Zaza. Also new is Singer Guy, as heard in the tender 'Gorik Pt. 2.' In the raw two-part track 'Vecht Voor Papier,' the flame of social commitment burns brightly, and in 'Papucho,' his agile flow shines once again. And those striking samples and tasteful start-stop beats! Zwangere Guy, we admire you, period. Photo by Laurens Leroy
