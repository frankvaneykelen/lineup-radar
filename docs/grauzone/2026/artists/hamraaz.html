We are delighted to welcome Hamraaz to Grauzone, a duo whose deeply moving exploration of modal and makam traditions creates a musical language that feels both ancient and strikingly contemporary. Formed in Rotterdam in 2021, Hamraaz brings together two extraordinary multi-instrumentalists: Lucie, who grew up with the traditional music of Auvergne and later immersed herself in modal traditions in Istanbul; and Khorshid Dadbeh, an Iranian musician trained in Tar, Setar, and Tanbour with a master's degree from Codarts and a strong mission to share and evolve Iranian musical heritage.
