Bio

The Etters began as a chaotic idea born from a midlife crisis—and quickly evolved into one of the most unmistakably fun garage-punk acts in the Netherlands. When vocalist Jerry (former singer of The Jerry Hormone Ego Trip) hit a crisis point at 42, his counselor offered two options: buy a Harley-Davidson, or start a loud band with two younger punk musicians. Broke but motivated, he chose the latter, teaming up with Luna (bass) and Kate Oram (Chattermag) (drums) to form The Etters.
