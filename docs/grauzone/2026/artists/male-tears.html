Festival Bio (English)

LA synth pop band Male Tears are described as a "goth's love affair with 1980s new wave," blending darkwave, goth rock, EBM, new romantic, and futurepop, creating a sound they call "Dark Rave." Releasing records on Avant Records (Nuovo Testamento, Buzz Kull, SDH, Qual), they began as a post-pandemic art project, founded by singer-songwriter James Edward, and have since mutated into a full-fledged weirdo-pop four-piece. Rooted in Edward's taste for irony, camp, and erratically applied makeup, the band delivers a playful yet biting take on underground music culture. Their sound merges synth-driven pop, goth undertones, and a flair for melodrama, balancing catchy hooks with a satirical edge.
