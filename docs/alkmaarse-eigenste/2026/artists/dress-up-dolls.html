Bio

Dress Up Dolls is an alternative rock trio from Alkmaar, Netherlands, featuring Yoram van der Reep on drums, Aris Rem on bass, and Daan Stam on vocals. Known for their powerful sound and energetic live performances, the band gained recognition in the Dutch music scene after winning the Rob Acda Award. They have played at major festivals such as Bevrijdingspop and NH-Pop Live, and their style draws comparisons to acts like Muse and Radiohead. In December 2025, Dress Up Dolls marked their reunion by performing new material at Alkmaars Eigenste Festival, signaling an exciting new chapter for the band.
