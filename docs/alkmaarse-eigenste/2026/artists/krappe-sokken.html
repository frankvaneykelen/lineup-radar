Bio

Krappe Sokken is a band from the Alkmaar and West Friesland region, recognized for their memorable song about the Karperton parking garage. Over time, their sound has evolved while their name—an expression from the local dialect meaning “just enough to make it work”—remains the same. In summer 2025, the band released their EP Saturday Spice, a reflection of their musical journey. Featuring five tracks with a cheerful and danceable character, the EP showcases the diverse influences each member brings during their weekly Saturday writing sessions.
