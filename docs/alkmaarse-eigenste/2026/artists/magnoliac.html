Bio

Magnoliac is a band that blends shoegaze, dream pop, and post-punk with a fresh, distinctive twist. Drawing inspiration from iconic acts like My Bloody Valentine, Slowdive, Interpol, and Joy Division, their music combines heavy, textured guitars with ethereal soundscapes and driving rhythms.
