Bio

Orchestre Partout is a Dutch music initiative founded in 2010 with the mission to connect people through the universal language of music. The organization brings together newcomers and Dutch musicians to create inclusive musical experiences. Through workshops, grassroots bands in refugee centers, professional ensembles, and collaborative projects, Orchestre Partout promotes cultural exchange and improvisation. Their activities range from intimate jams and family concerts to large-scale collaborations with renowned ensembles, all aimed at celebrating diversity and fostering creativity.
