Festival Bio (English)

These men from Cornwall make tasteful choices in everything they do. Even their band name, both graceful and rugged, is a gem of a metaphor. Or consider their sought-after T-shirts, tours with Fontaines D.C., and the noisy '90s sound on their second album 'Midas,' recorded in the studio where Nirvana created their masterpiece 'In Utero': you can hear it, you can feel it. But even though Jacob Slater and his crew sometimes push sound systems to their limits, a tear of emotion is always close at hand. It is precisely this vulnerable contrast that makes Wunderhorse so beloved.
