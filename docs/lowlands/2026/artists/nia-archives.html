As a third-generation British Jamaican, Dehaney Nia Lishahn Hunt grows up in a musical household: her family runs a studio, radio station, and sound system filled with dance and gospel. Leaving home at just sixteen, the creative talent begins building her career as a DJ/producer, with a sparkling vocal talent as an added bonus.
