Maribou State is a British electronic music duo known for their fusion of downtempo beats, soulful melodies, and organic instrumentation. Festival Bio (English)

Those seeking both melancholy and euphoria will find themselves perfectly catered to by the finely tuned music of the electronic indie-pop duo Maribou State. Warm-blooded music where downtempo, trip-hop, and a generous dash of soul play tag, all driven by glowing melodies, now spanning three albums. Live, the Londoners expand into a five-piece band, complete with a fairy-tale vocalist and optionally enhanced with a string section, choir, dancers (as seen at Glastonbury last year), and/or a full orchestra. Absolutely nothing is spared to bring you to a state of ultimate bliss.
