Festival Bio (English)

If there’s no collaboration with greats like Florence Welch, A$AP Rocky, Alex Turner, or FKA twigs on the agenda, multi-instrumentalist Dev Hynes channels his songwriting fantasies into Blood Orange: R&B meets indie meets warm synth-pop. In the style of Prince, but enriched with timeless accents from Strauss and Bach. Leading up to the 2025 release of the album ‘Essex Honey,’ the Londoner experienced a period of mourning, which he processes in his signature orchestral style. Live, it all hits as powerfully as it does angelically.
