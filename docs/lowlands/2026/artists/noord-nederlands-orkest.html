Bio

The Noord Nederlands Orkest (NNO) is one of the oldest and most prominent orchestras in the Netherlands, known for its performances of classical and contemporary works. Based in Groningen, the orchestra collaborates with renowned conductors and soloists, offering a wide range of symphonic music to audiences across the northern Netherlands.

Grand music deserves a grand stage. The North Netherlands Orchestra (NNO) and Concert Choir deliver a moshpit-proof performance of the 'Requiem' by Italian composer Giuseppe Verdi. This 1874 masterpiece is a mass for the dead, and 'requiem' is Latin for 'rest,' but the NNO turns it into a lively affair. Chief conductor Eivind Gullberg Jensen knows exactly how to enchant Lowlands and won't hesitate to aim his baton at the audience. Let Verdi's classical banger blow you away.
