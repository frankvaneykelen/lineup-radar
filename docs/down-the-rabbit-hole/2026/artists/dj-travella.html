DJ Travella is a young producer from Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, known for his innovative take on singeli, a high-tempo electronic dance music genre. His tracks blend frenetic beats with experimental soundscapes, pushing the boundaries of traditional East African music.

Festival Bio (English)

Like fellow DJ Tobzy, DJ Travella is a welcome guest at Nyege Nyege, the festival and label from Uganda. Travella himself hails from Tanzania, where, as a young upstart in Dar es Salaam, he was captivated by the breakneck rhythms of singeli. With an average of 200-300 beats per minute, singeli is already one of the fastest music genres in the world, but Travella's hyperactive take takes it to the next level: this is singeli on steroids, with distorted synths and samples and relentless, pounding drums that drag you to the dance floor without mercy.
