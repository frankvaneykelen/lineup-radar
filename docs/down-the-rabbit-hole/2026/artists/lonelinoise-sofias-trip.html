Thomas Dulfer, bassoonist and director of the contemporary classical music platform Lonelinoise, introduces you to the lives of two master composers: the Iranian Soheil Shayesteh, who clashes traditional, Eastern kamancheh sounds with trance, and Sofia Gubaidulina, the Russian conservatory rebel who spent her life swimming against the culture-suffocating Soviet laws—and even survived a KGB assassination attempt. The production "Sofia's Trip" brings their brave, creative worlds together in a psychedelic adventure full of unexpected twists. No dress code, no program booklets, no folding chairs; just forty-five minutes of breathless admiration.
