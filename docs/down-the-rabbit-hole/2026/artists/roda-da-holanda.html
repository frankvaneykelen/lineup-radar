And now for something completely different: no DJ, no band, but a Brazilian live party with musicians gathered around a table, the roda da samba. Roda da Holanda is the Dutch version, with lead singer Femke Smit as the eye-catcher and, in addition to samba, other Brazilian styles like forró, choro, and bossa nova on the menu. It may read as something unique, but it sounds flawless thanks to an extensive parade of instruments, including a generous array of percussion, perfect for festive drumming and singing at the top of your lungs. Perfect for a lively dance party, with nothing but happy faces. Carnival in the Green Hills!
