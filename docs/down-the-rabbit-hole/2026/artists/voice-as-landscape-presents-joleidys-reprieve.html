By Alec Mateo featuring Soraya Lutangu Bonaventure, and S*an D. Henry-Smith Words connect, comfort, and can provide strength and essential grounding in turbulent times. ‘Joleidy’s Reprieve’ is a new project by Alec Mateo under the banner of Voice as Landscape, the forward-thinking performance platform with a deep love for language, which gives voices a stage—and vice versa. For this project, the American co-founder and artist invited theater maker Soraya Lutangu Bonaventure and poet/photographer S*an D. Henry-Smith, two like-minded experimental spirits who had previously collaborated with Voice as Landscape during the two-day project ‘Borrowing a Distant Hill.’
