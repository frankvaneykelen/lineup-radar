Bio

Joy Crookes is a British singer-songwriter known for her soulful voice and introspective lyrics, blending influences from jazz, R&B, and South Asian music. She has received critical acclaim for her debut album 'Skin' and was nominated for the 2020 BRIT Rising Star Award.

Festival Bio (English)

She had been out of the spotlight for a while, Joy Crookes, the London singer of Bengali-Irish descent who debuted in 2021 with a stunning neo-soul album and was already a highlight at Down The Rabbit Hole a year later. But now she's back. That life hasn't always smiled upon her in the meantime becomes clear from the emotional lyrics on ‘Juniper’ (2025), an album that continues where its predecessor left off with retro soul infused with touches of trip-hop, rap, and reggae. Jazzier than Amy Winehouse, more hip-hop than Celeste, a true ode to Joy.
