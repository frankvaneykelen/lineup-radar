Bio

A master of blending house, disco, and techno with a vintage touch.

Massimiliano Pagliara is an Italian DJ and producer known for his eclectic mix of house, disco, and techno, often infused with retro and cosmic influences. Based in Berlin, he has released critically acclaimed albums on labels like Live At Robert Johnson and is a regular performer at renowned clubs such as Panorama Bar.

Festival Bio (English)

In the grimiest corners of techno, the joy sometimes threatens to vanish, as if clubbing is secretly a punishment. In this sense, Massimiliano Pagliara comes to your rescue. The Berlin-based Italian understands that even the toughest set can sparkle with touches of italo, deep house, and pop. Versatility runs in the blood of this synthesizer aficionado, who can infuse the analog warmth of yesteryear into hard-hitting bangers. His beloved albums are a nostalgic listening journey between hard and soft, while his dynamic sets always keep the tempo delightfully high. Image by: Alessio Boni
