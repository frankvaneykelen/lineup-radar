Festival Bio (English)

Hibotep is yet another crown jewel of the Nyege Nyege collective from Uganda. The Somali artist is active as a DJ, producer, and fashion designer, has a knack for rapping, directs music videos, and creates art installations. As a selector, she is just as versatile, delivering intense, unpredictable dance sets where she skillfully blends traditional African styles with techno, grime, drum and bass, and trap—her great love, as Hibotep was once aptly dubbed the Trap Priestess. And you know the old saying in The Bizarre: those who sow bass shall reap ecstasy. Image by Picass Art
