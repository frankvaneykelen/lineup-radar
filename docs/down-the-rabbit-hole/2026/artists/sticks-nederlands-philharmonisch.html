If there is one Dutch-speaking rapper who could claim the title of GOAT, it’s Sticks. Having started in the groundbreaking rap crew Opgezwolle, he went on to achieve solo success and built exciting supergroups like Fakkelbrigade and Great Minds, alongside powerhouse colleagues such as Typhoon and Jiggy Djé. In addition, the influential rapper from Zwolle has delved into a wide range of activities, from serving as an ambassador for football club PEC Zwolle to founding the hip-hop platform Fakkelteitgroep, and from working as an A&R executive at record label Top Notch to creating a multimedia art project. In short, the ideal Special Guest at Down The Rabbit Hole, who will not only perform a show at Hotot but also pop up in the most unexpected places around the Groene Heuvels all weekend long. Not to be missed: his Friday afternoon show at Hotot, where Sticks, together with the majestic Netherlands Philharmonic Orchestra, will perform a set of hip-hop bangers specially arranged for the symphony orchestra.
