About (from Festival)

The South London rapper Loyle Carner is a wordsmith of the kind you encounter only a few times per generation. His music is a smooth amalgam of soul, jazz, and soft rock, the latter since his most recent album 'Hopefully !' – a record on which the thirty-something audibly grapples with the do's and don'ts of newfound fatherhood. A theme well-suited to his confessional rap, full of poetic insights and a calm flow, which recently earned him a headline spot at Glastonbury and a double sold-out AFAS Live.
