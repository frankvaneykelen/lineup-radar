If there is one Dutch-speaking rapper who deserves to be considered for the title of GOAT, it’s Sticks. Once part of the groundbreaking rap crew Opgezwolle, he went on to have a successful solo career and built exciting supergroups like Fakkelbrigade and Great Minds, alongside fellow heavyweights such as Typhoon and Jiggy Djé. Additionally, the influential artist from Zwolle has delved into a wide range of activities, from being an ambassador for the PEC Zwolle football club to founding the hip-hop platform Fakkelteitgroep, and from working as an A&R representative at the record label Top Notch to creating a multimedia art project. In short, the perfect Special Guest for Down The Rabbit Hole, where, in addition to a show at Hotot, he will pop up in the most unexpected places around the Groene Heuvels all weekend long.
