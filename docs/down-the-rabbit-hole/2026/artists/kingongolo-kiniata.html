About (from Festival)

These gentlemen from Kinshasa turn junk into gold. In their hands, recycled everyday objects transform into versatile instruments for explosive punky electronics with a touch of Afropop. Kin'Gongolo Kiniata are the torchbearers of the new Congolese music: rooted in tradition, with a raised fist towards a groundbreaking new sound. On stage, they give a voice to the vibrant streets of Kinshasa through provocative songs about poverty, war, and survival. When life gives you lemons?
