Bio

About (from Festival)

Little girls grow up. Little Simz, the London artist, rapper, and actress born Simbi Ajikawo, has undoubtedly evolved into Big Simz fifteen years after her first mixtapes. A journey that received a significant boost with her breakthrough album *Sometimes I Might Be Introvert* (2021), filled with tracks that have since become classics, such as "Woman" and "Point and Kill," also thanks to the soaring vocals of soul companion Obongjayar. Two albums later, Simz is now an undeniable shining star in a musical universe all her own, a guiding light for little girls everywhere. Photo by Thibaut Grevet
