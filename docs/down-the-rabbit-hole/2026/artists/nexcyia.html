Festival Bio (English)

We often associate ambient music with a kind of musical wallpaper: unobtrusive sounds to drift away to. NEXCYIA sees it differently. His ambient resembles modernist sculptures: stunning sonic creations made of raw materials and strange textures. The American artist molds musical worlds that explore the tension between beauty and unease. His album *Endless Path of Memory* is composed of fluid tracks that comfort but also creep under your skin. Wonderfully disorienting music, then, which at RADIATE VI invites you to get lost and drift even deeper into reverie.

Heerlijk desoriënterende muziek dus, die op RADIATE VI uitnodigt om te verdwalen en nog dieper weg te dromen.
