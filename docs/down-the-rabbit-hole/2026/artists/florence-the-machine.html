Background

Florence + The Machine is a British band led by Florence Welch, known for their powerful vocals, poetic lyrics, and a blend of indie rock and baroque pop. They have achieved critical and commercial success with albums like 'Lungs' and 'Ceremonials,' earning multiple awards and nominations.

About (from Festival)

Florence Welch is striving with all her might to reconcile body and mind. Like a musical exorcist, she delves into the world of witchcraft and folkloric horror, resulting in her latest album, ‘Everybody Scream’. The red-haired fury Welch sings and screams her way to healing, as on ‘One of the Greats’, where that unmistakable voice shines beautifully against a dark, grungy guitar chord backdrop. It tastes bittersweet and leaves you wanting more. Look forward to a more diverse yet familiarly exuberant setlist: Florence + The Machine is a universally beloved, raw, and incredibly powerful headliner. Photo by De Wilde
