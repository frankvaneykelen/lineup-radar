Bio

Caribbean chaos, live and unleashed

Using festival bio due to a lack of publicly available data

For Rozaly, the exuberant micro-dance genres of the Caribbean are cultural heritage of the highest order. His life mission is to map out all these diverse riddim styles. The archivist/DJ/producer presents the absolute highlights of what he calls "Carnival Ganza": euphoric soca, hyperactive Dennery segment, and libidinous bouyon. The latter style serves as the foundation for his new formation. RIDDIMBOX is a fully improvisational live band featuring disorienting melodies, insane tempos from percussionist Nicolini (Devon Rexi), and electrifying vocals from Amsterdam-based MC Toff Youth.
