About (from Festival)

Producer Paul Epworth (Florence + The Machine, Bloc Party, Adele) had a revelation during Baxter Dury's performance at Glastonbury 2024. He knew exactly how Dury should sound on his next album. At The Church Studios, Dury's material for 'Allbarone' silently found its way to the trash, and his narrative vocals—practically spoon-fed to him by his father Ian—were unleashed in a new habitat. More intense, more electric, more danceable – yet still with an exceptionally funky sense of rhythm and liberating choruses as key ingredients. Speaking of revelations. Photo by Tom Beard
