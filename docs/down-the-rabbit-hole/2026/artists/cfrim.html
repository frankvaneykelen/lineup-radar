Festival Bio (English)

Because there wasn’t much to do in the suburbs of Melbourne, Charlotte Frimpong spent her teenage years as a kickflipping skater. Until she discovered club music. What started with mixing YouTube videos evolved into the alias C. FRIM. Today, the renowned DJ honors her Ghanaian-Filipino roots with an explosive fusion of global club sounds. She blends the pounding drums of gqom, hard drum, dancehall, jungle, and countless other underground styles into overwhelming, mind-blowing sets. Moreover, as a co-founder of the queer platform Dutty Worldwide, she advocates for radical inclusivity on dance floors where boundaries blur.
