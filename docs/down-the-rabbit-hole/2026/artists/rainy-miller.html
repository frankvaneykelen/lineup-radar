Festival Bio (English)

For the producer, songwriter, and singer Rainy Miller from Preston, drill serves as an expressive canvas for deeply personal music. At times, his simmering blend of pop, ambient, rap, and pounding sub-bass is overwhelmingly intense, but through this musical turbulence, the artist wrestles with his inner demons in the most intimate way. Rainy Miller's voice, often distorted by AutoTune, resembles a lament—one through which Miller delivers urgent commentary on a bleak existence in the gritty north of England. Image by: Jack Bowes
