Festival Bio (English)

Yuval Avital understands that a DJ must find the perfect marriage between hard and soft. Behind the decks, this pride of Arnhem delivers a mix of gritty house bangers and ecstatic disco bursting with euphoric vocals. With a raw underground edge layered over soulful, funky grooves, her sets take you back to the roots: the historic warehouse raves in Chicago. Her knack for authentic house music has also taken Yuval Avital to many extraordinary places: using Arnhem as her springboard, she has already graced the CDJs in Panama and Colombia, among others.
