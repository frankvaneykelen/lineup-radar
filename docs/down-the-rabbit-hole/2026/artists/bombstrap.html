Rotterdam and Manchester have a new city connection: BOMBSTRAP. These troublemakers adhere to two truths. 1. There's plenty wrong with society, and 2. Nothing beats playing live. The first is expertly shredded into hellish vocals, driven at breakneck speed by deafening guitar and drum violence. The second is captured on their debut album ‘LIVE AT WORM,’ crafted as it should be in DIY hardcore punk circles: flyering the venue, plugging in, and hitting record. Medium or well done? No, BOMBSTRAP devours the stage raw and spits out a furious mosh pit. Burp.
