Bio

Using festival bio due to a lack of publicly available data

If your starting point is surgical drum-'n-bass, you know exactly how deep the blade can cut. Zohar achieves this with insanely abstract club bangers, where the drums ricochet back and forth and the low end just keeps rattling on. It feels as if you're in a pressure chamber and the walls suddenly start closing in on you. This artistic interpretation of boundary-pushing club music is something Zohar pursues not only through their own ZHR label but also with ventures into the world of conceptual sound art and film, exploring the gray area between the club and installation art.
