Festival Bio (English)

Rafa Andrade grew up with fiery baile funk blasting through the streets of São Paulo from passing cars. In his razor-sharp tracks, BADSISTA elevates Brazilian street culture to a higher level by injecting minimalist club music with the adrenaline of ghetto, gabber, trance, and hip-hop. Today, BADSISTA brings his gritty and uncompromising Brazilian sound to dance floors worldwide. As the founder of the feminist collective Bandida, this trans man also empowers a growing number of talented queer DJs and producers from marginalized communities.
