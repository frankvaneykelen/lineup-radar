Festival Bio (English)

DTRH house friends De Staat are known as one of Europe’s most inventive and fearless live acts. For over a quarter of a century, they have consistently pushed the boundaries of what a rock band can be. They combine razor-sharp social commentary with a unique visual stage language – ‘Witch Doctor,’ anyone? – and a genre-defying sound that, besides being on your airplay, has also featured in hit games and global campaigns. Just check out their trophy cabinet, gleaming with accolades like Edison Awards, 3FM Awards, and a Bernays Lifetime Achievement Award. All of this has taken the internationally renowned band to every corner of the world: from SXSW, Glastonbury, Lowlands, Sziget, Pukkelpop, Rock Werchter, and DEICHBRAND to tours with bands like The Rolling Stones, Muse, and Biffy Clyro. In 2026, Torre Florim and his companions will return with "De Staat Becomes De Staat," a grand new project centered around a fresh album, live show, and a massive art installation that will once again raise the bar. Unthinkable? With De Staat, you can even consider the impossible possible.
