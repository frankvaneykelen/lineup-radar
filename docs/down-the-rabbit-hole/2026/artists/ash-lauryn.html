Festival Bio (English)

For Detroit-born and Atlanta-based Ash Lauryn, club culture is about more than just serving the dance floor. As the head of the multidisciplinary platform Underground & Black, she sees this culture as a true cornerstone of a community—a place for sharing stories and celebrating identity. Her delightfully soulful house and sultry techno serve as a beautiful vehicle for an even deeper experience, one where connection takes center stage. So, enjoy her summery sets and feel, even for a moment, at one with all the other beautiful people on the dance floor.
