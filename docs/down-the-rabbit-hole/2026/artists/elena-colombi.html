Festival Bio (English)

That the countless strange mutations of contemporary club music can form a beautifully organic whole is something Elena Colombi demonstrates with every set. The NTS resident and head of the Osàre! Editions label is a master at blending styles, with a particular penchant for obscure gems from yet-to-be-discovered artists. The Italian-born Londoner serves as the connecting thread between industrial rhythms, pulsating techno, atmospheric ambient, freaky disco, and a whole treasure trove of other mind-blowing club styles. Behind the decks, anything is possible, making an Elena Colombi set always a promise of immersion into the unknown.
