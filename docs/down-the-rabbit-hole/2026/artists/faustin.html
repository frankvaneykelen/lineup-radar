Patience is a virtue. Just ask Faustin, who gradually unfolds his DJ sets into a musical fairytale that intoxicates you with happiness. Driven by endlessly looping Afro percussion, the Rotterdam native of Antillean descent weaves a narrative nourished by house, techno, ambient, and trance. This makes Faustin a sought-after name, a proud representative of the queer community, regularly featured at Operator Radio and in clubs like POING, SPIELRAUM, and Club RAUM, while venues like Kunsthal and Worm have hosted his audiovisual performances.
