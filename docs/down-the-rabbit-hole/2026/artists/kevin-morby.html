Background

Kevin Morby is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist known for his introspective lyrics and a blend of indie rock and folk influences. He has released several critically acclaimed albums, including 'Singing Saw' and 'City Music', which showcase his poetic storytelling and unique sound.

My Take

Kevin Morby is widely praised for his evocative songwriting and ability to craft atmospheric, emotionally resonant music. His live performances are often described as intimate and captivating, making him a standout in the indie music scene.

About (from Festival)

A song by Kevin Morby is the musical equivalent of flipping through a photo album filled with old Polaroids. The singer-songwriter, who grew up in Kansas and moved to New York, always has one foot in the past. This makes him one of the few contemporary musicians who truly earns comparisons to Bob Dylan and Leonard Cohen. However, it’s not pure nostalgia: in his sultry songs, this troubadour reflects on our tumultuous times. They are passionate odes to the desolate places and lonely people of the modern world.
