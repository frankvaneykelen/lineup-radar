Festival Bio (English)

The Isaan region in Thailand is the birthplace of molam, a unique genre that combines Thai folk with psychedelic funk. Led by DJ Maft Sai, The Paradise Bangkok Molam International Band has been putting this relatively unknown genre on the map for over a decade. PBMIB's adventurous mix of funk, rock, electronic, folk, and psychedelia transcends borders and is gaining an increasingly larger stage: the Thai gentlemen have already opened for Damon Albarn and can be heard in the third season of 'The White Lotus'.
