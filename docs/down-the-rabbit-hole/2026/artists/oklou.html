Festival Bio (English)

Somewhere between healing ambient, sparkling hyperpop, and invigorating club music lies the emotional core of Oklou, who has quickly become one of the most important songwriters of our time. If the mixtape 'Galore' (2020) was a convincing preview of her unique productions and dreamy vocal style, then debut album 'choke enough' (2025) is her magnum opus. Few artists manage to strike such a perfect balance between the everyday and the celestial, two extremes that Oklou skillfully navigates with her silky voice in intimate gems.
