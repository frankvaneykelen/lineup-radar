Festival Bio (English)

This trumpeter, DJ, and producer has spent his entire life gazing at the stars. As a child, Kareem Ali dreamed of becoming a rocket scientist. Now, he creates cosmic deep house, inspired by Afrofuturist icons like Sun Ra and the cult techno group Drexciya. However, his greatest role model is Miles Davis, the genius who rewrote all the rules of jazz. This inspired Kareem Ali to merge his trumpet playing on stage with electronic music. Thanks to this unique blend of stratospheric house and analog sounds, this hyper-productive musician is regarded as the most promising rising talent in the American club scene.
