Festival Bio (English)

Arp Frique is not one to be easily pinned down, let alone boxed in. In addition to his regular show at Down The Rabbit Hole with gospel-funk sensation The Perpetual Singers, he’s treating The Bizarre to a DJ set packed with fine tunes from his personal record collection. From it, the selector/producer predominantly picks chic, freaky dancefloor gems from hot regions like Ghana and Cape Verde, so expect a fiery dose of spice in your sweat. Because sweat you will—on Arp Frique’s dance floor, the thermostat is always set to disco inferno.
