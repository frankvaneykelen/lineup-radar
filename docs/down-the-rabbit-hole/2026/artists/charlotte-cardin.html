About (from Festival)

Mon dieu! The most-played French-language track on French radio and TV was created by a Canadian? With "Feel Good" (2024), Charlotte Cardin from Montréal firmly established her presence on the European mainland and beyond. She brings along sublime alternative pop, continuously surprising with perfectly balanced synths, beats, and guitars. The same goes for Cardin's lyrics, where she tackles every subject and personal detail in both French and English—sometimes seductive, other times confrontational, but always spot on.
