Bio

Innovative producer blending experimental electronic textures with emotional depth.

Vegyn is a London-based electronic music producer known for his genre-defying soundscapes, blending glitchy beats, ambient textures, and emotional melodies. He gained recognition for his collaborations with Frank Ocean on albums like 'Blonde' and 'Endless' and has released acclaimed solo projects such as 'Only Diamonds Cut Diamonds'.

Festival Bio (English)

‘Enigma’ sums up Vegyn perfectly, as Londoner Joseph Thornalley prefers to avoid the spotlight.
